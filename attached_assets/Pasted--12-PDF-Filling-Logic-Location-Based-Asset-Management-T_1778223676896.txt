## 12: PDF Filling Logic (Location-Based)
Asset Management: The system uses the original, unmodified PDF forms (AKD-01 to AKD-06) as base assets
.
Coordinate Mapping: Each form field is manually mapped to specific (X, Y) coordinates on an A4 canvas (595 x 842 points).
Layering Mechanism: The backend uses ReportLab to generate a transparent PDF layer containing the student's data at the mapped coordinates. This layer is then merged with the original form using pypdf [PRD Section 7.6, 12].
Mapping Responsibility: The developer is responsible for identifying the exact alignment for every field, including text strings, checkmarks (ticks), and official remarks [User Request].

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Manual Mapping Requirements (AKD-01 to AKD-06)
You will need to determine the (X, Y) coordinates for the following fields across all documents found in the sources:
1. Global Student Profile (Common to Most Forms)
{Student_Name}: Full Name of the student
.
{Matric_No}: Student ID/Matriculation number
.
{Programme}: Course or Program of study
.
{Phone_No}: Mobile or contact number
.
{Address}: Residential or mailing address
.
2. Form-Specific Request Data
PPST/AKD-01 (Withdrawal)
Reason Ticks: Coordinates for the three boxes: "Received job offer," "Transferring institution," and "Personal problems"
.
{Institution_Name}: Text field for the name of the new institution
.
{Student_Signature_Date}: Date of the request
.
PPST/AKD-02 (Exam Appeal)
{Semester} / {Session}: The academic period for the appeal
.
Reason Ticks: Coordinates for "Illness," "Death," or "Failed Course"
.
Course Table (Dynamic Rows): Coordinates for Course Code, Title, and Original Exam Date/Time
.
PPST/AKD-03 (Result Review)
Payment Details: {Receipt_No}, {Receipt_Date}, and {Amount_Paid}
.
{Course_Table}: Code, Title, Lecturer Name, Grade, and Offering Faculty
.
PPST/AKD-04 (Absence Justification)
{Reason_Text}: The detailed explanation for missing class
.
{Date_of_Absence}: Specific date(s) the student was away
.
Class Table: Course Code, Name, and Class Type (Lecture/Tutorial/Practical)
.
PPST/AKD-05 (Room Booking)
{Applicant_Position}: Student’s role or job title
.
Room Selection (Ticks/Circles): Coordinates for individual rooms: BK (1-4), BT PPST (1-5), and BT Annex (6-9)
.
{Purpose} / {Booking_Date}: The reason and date for the reservation
.
PPST/AKD-06 (Sick Leave)
{IC_No}: Student's Identification Card number
.
{Study_Group}: Lecture, Tutorial, or Practical group identifier
.
Facility Tick: Government vs. Private Hospital/Clinic
.
Class Tick: Lecture, Tutorial, or Practical checkbox
.
3. Official Use Data (Post-Approval Mapping)
For every form, you must also map locations for Admin and Director inputs:
{Admin_Remarks}: Initial review comments
.
{Director_Remarks}: Final comments from the Pengarah
.
Approval Ticks: Specific coordinates for "Approved" (Diluluskan) or "Not Approved" boxes
.
{Decision_Date}: The final date the status was updated
.
